Our Refund Policy

We want you to love SpellRightPro. If you don't, we'll make it right.

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee — No Questions Asked

Our 14-Day Guarantee

If you are not completely satisfied with your SpellRightPro Premium subscription within 14 days of your purchase date, we will issue a full refund — no questions asked, no hassle.

We believe in our product and want you to feel confident upgrading. If it is not the right fit for you, simply reach out and we will process your refund promptly.

What Is Eligible for a Refund

  • Monthly subscriptions — full refund if requested within 14 days of the original purchase date.
  • Annual subscriptions — full refund if requested within 14 days of the original purchase date.
  • First-time purchases — all new subscribers are covered by the 14-day guarantee.
  • Renewals after 14 days — automatic renewal charges after the initial 14-day window are not eligible for a refund unless there was a billing error. Contact us and we will review.
  • Partial-month refunds — we do not issue pro-rated refunds for unused time beyond the 14-day window.

How to Request a Refund

The process is simple and takes less than 2 minutes:

  1. 1
    Email us at spellrightpro@gmail.com with the subject line: "Refund Request".
  2. 2
    Include your purchase details: the email address used to sign up and the approximate date of purchase. A receipt or order number helps but is not required.
  3. 3
    We will confirm your refund request by email within 1 business day.
  4. 4
    Your refund is processed within 5–7 business days back to the original payment method. Your subscription will also be cancelled at this point.

Billing Errors & Disputes

If you believe you were charged in error — for example, a duplicate charge or a charge after cancellation — please contact us immediately at spellrightpro@gmail.com. We will investigate and resolve billing errors regardless of the 14-day window.

We strongly encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank, as we can typically resolve issues faster and more smoothly directly.

Cancelling Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancelling stops future billing but does not automatically trigger a refund for the current billing period (unless you are within the 14-day window).

To cancel, email spellrightpro@gmail.com or use the cancellation link in any of your subscription receipts. We will confirm cancellation within 1 business day.

After cancellation, your Premium access continues until the end of the current billing period.
