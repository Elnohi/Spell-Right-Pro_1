Refund Policy

Last updated: 25 Aug 2025

Subscriptions

SpellRightPro Premium is a subscription billed in advance (monthly or annually). You may cancel any time from the Manage Billing page or by contacting support. When canceled, your access remains active until the end of the current billing period.

Refunds

  • First-time purchases: If Premium hasn’t been meaningfully used, you may request a refund within 7 days of purchase.
  • Renewals: Refunds for renewals are generally not issued once the period starts. We’ll review exceptional cases (accidental renewal, duplicate charge, etc.).
  • Technical issues: If you experience a service problem that we can’t resolve promptly, we’ll offer a prorated or full refund depending on impact.

How to request

Contact us via Contact Support with your account email and reason. Most refunds are processed by Stripe to your original payment method within 5–10 business days after approval.

Changes

We may revise this policy. We’ll post updates here with a new “Last updated” date.
